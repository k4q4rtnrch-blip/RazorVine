Studio

RazorVine exists in the space between organic classiness and loud expressions. Every piece is built to carry weight — Both with the design and the story behind it. No mass production. No dilution.

Designed and forged in-house by Joseph J Nichols.

Commissions process

01

Free WhatsApp Consultation.

Concepts, references and sizings are taken. No financial obligation. Enquiry details are in the contact section.

02

Design, calculations and material sourcing.

Free sketches and 3d renderings are made, refined and approved. After which, deposit is paid for the materials used

03

Forged in-house.

Crafted, approved by you, and delivered either in person or via secure postage.
